This is how we make

our oatmilk

Hey! We see that you’re curious about our manufacturing process. So here it is! The process is very simple and we ensure that you get the best quality product and we cause the least amount of harm to the environment.

MILLING

Milling is the very first stage in which we collect the finest quality oats and these raw oats are milled, hulled and washed to remove any unwanted substances from them.

MIXING WATER

This is the second stage after we’ve milled the finest oats for you. Water is added to the oats to make them into a soft, gooey mixture which helps liquefy the oats easily.

ENZYMING

Enzyming is an essential stage and in this stage we add natural enzymes to the oats that help break the starch present in them into smaller components namely malt sugar/maltose. This helps provide a natural sweetness to the product.

FILTERATION

This stage is crucial because we remove the unwanted elements like the loose shells.

BOTTLING

And finally, in the last stage we bottle the healthy OatMlk into beautiful, recyclable and reusable glass bottles to cause least harm to the environment.

HEAT TREATMENT

Heat Treatment is essential to ensure an extended shelf life of our products. It is done with pasteurization or with UHT.

INGREDIENTS

This is the fun part. In this stage we add di erent ingredients important for the product. These ingredients are essential fats, oils, vitamins and calcium.
